Research Question

"Do composite fundamental scores derived from publicly available financial ratios correlate with one-year price returns among NSE-listed Indian companies?"

This platform operationalises the hypothesis by building a scoring engine across six fundamental pillars, applying it to 36 NSE companies, and testing correlation with FY25 price returns in the Backtest section.

Limitations
Point-in-time data only — does not capture intra-year volatility or restatements
Pillar weights are subjectively determined; Sensitivity Analysis section tests robustness
Banking sector metrics (no current ratio) create asymmetric scoring — addressed via N/A exclusion
Sample of 36 companies limits statistical significance of backtest findings
Does not account for qualitative factors: management quality, ESG, regulatory risk
